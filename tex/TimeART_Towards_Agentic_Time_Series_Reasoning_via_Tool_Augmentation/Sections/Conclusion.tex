\section{Conclusion}
To sum up, we introduce an agentic framework for time series reasoning named TimeART in this work, which integrates multiple strong out-of-the-box analytical tools, including statistical functions to time series foundation models. To mitigate the common dilemmas in conventional training strategies, we propose to train TSRMs on expert trajectories and their own early experience, with a \textit{100k} corpus TimeToolBench released and a novel four-stage training strategy. Experimentally, we finetune an 8B TSRM equipped with TimeART, which achieves SOTA performance on multiple benchmarks, thus demonstrating the effectiveness of TimeART, TimeToolBench, and the proposed training strategy. In the future work, we plan to explore an efficient mechanism to enable LLMs to sense the time series with numerous variables and excessive long lengths.
